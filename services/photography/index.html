Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a photo shoot?

+
Every shoot includes pre-production planning, on-location photography, professional retouching, and delivery in multiple formats optimized for web, print, and social media.

How many final images will we receive?

+
This depends on the scope, but typically 30-50 final edited images per shoot day. We focus on quality over quantity.

Do you provide styling?

+
Yes, we provide art direction and can coordinate with stylists for lifestyle shoots. We'll advise on props, wardrobe, and scene setup.

Can you combine photo and video?

+
Absolutely. We often bundle photography with video production for maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
