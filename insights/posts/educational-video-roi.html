Why Cinematic Video is the Highest ROI Asset for 2026

By Reuben Hester • December 29, 2025

In the world of luxury marketing, "stopping the scroll" is no longer enough. The real metric of success is emotion. Why? Because emotion drives perceived value, and perceived value drives price elasticity.

The 2025 Video Shift: By the Numbers

For high-ticket brands, static images have hit a ceiling. While they are crucial for catalog sales, they fail to convey the feeling of ownership. According to 2025 marketing benchmarks, video ads are now generating over 3x more leads and 25% more impressions than static images for premium brands.

Furthermore, consistent video implementation is projected to drive 93% positive ROI for marketers this year. At Nusa & North, we see this daily: Campaigns utilizing narrative-driven, cinematic video assets see a significantly higher contribution margin compared to static-only campaigns for products priced over €500.

Brand Equity vs. Direct Response

The old dogma was that you had to choose: Build brand equity OR drive direct response sales. This is false. High-quality video production actually lowers your Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) over time because it builds trust faster.

With video traffic expected to make up 82% of all internet traffic by late 2025, the brands that win will be those that master "Performance storytelling"—using the "hook-retain-reward" structure of performance marketing but executing it with the aesthetic precision of a Vogue editorial.
