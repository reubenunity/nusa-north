The "Welcome Flow" Worth €50k/Month

By Reuben Hester • Dec 15, 2025

Most brands treat their Welcome Flow like a transaction: "Here is your 10% off code, please buy something." This is a massive missed opportunity. For high-ticket and luxury brands, the Welcome Flow is your digital showroom tour. It's where you build the brand equity that justifies your price point.

1. The Benchmark: €21.18 Per Recipient?

When a user signs up for your newsletter, their intent is at its peak. Yet most brands squander this with a visible discount code. According to the latest Klaviyo Luxury Benchmarks (2025), the top 10% of fashion brands are achieving a staggering €21.18 Revenue Per Recipient (RPR) on their Welcome Series.

Compare that to the average of ~€2.65. The difference? The top performers treat the Welcome Flow as an onboarding experience, not a coupon delivery system.

2. The 4-Email "Brand Equity" Sequence

Instead of a single blast, we implement a narrative arc:

  • Email 1: The "Why" (Immediate): Delivers the incentive (if any) subtly, but focuses 80% on the brand's origin story and mission. Why do you exist?
  • Email 2: The "How" (Day 2): A behind-the-scenes look at craftsmanship or process. Show the materials, the studio, the people. Establish quality.
  • Email 3: The "Who" (Day 4): Social proof. Not just 5-star reviews, but UGC, user stories, and press features. "Others validate your choice."
  • Email 4: The "What" (Day 7): Product education. Deep dive into a best-seller, explaining features that justify the investment.

3. Segmentation is Key

Not all leads are equal. We segment this flow based on traffic source. A lead coming from a "Sustainable Fashion" blog post should receive a different email copy than one coming from a generic Facebook ad. Personalization at this early stage drastically reduces unsubscribe rates and increases conversion.

"Email is the only channel you own. Don't rent your audience's attention—earn it with value."
