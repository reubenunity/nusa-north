Editorial Precision: Producing for Harper's Bazaar

By Reuben Hester • December 28, 2025

In collaboration with Harper’s Bazaar and Schwarzkopf, we were tasked with producing a "What’s in My Bag" episode featured international actress Diane Kruger. This wasn't just a simple interview; it was a collision of three major brand identities.

Full-Scope Production

Our team handled the full scope of production—from directing and lighting to sound design and cinematography. The challenge with high-fashion editorial pieces is balancing the natural charisma of the talent with the strict aesthetic requirements of a global publication.

We opted for a lighting setup that mimicked natural daylight but with controllable soft fills to ensure Diane looked flawless yet approachable. The set design was kept minimal to keep the focus entirely on the storytelling and the subtle product placements required by Schwarzkopf.

The Result

The final deliverable was a sleek, high-fashion editorial piece that aligned seamlessly with Harper’s Bazaar’s global standard. It serves as a case study in how we merge celebrity storytelling with luxury brand partnerships, creating content that feels authentic to the audience while hitting every commercial beat for the stakeholders.
