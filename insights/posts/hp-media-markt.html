Elevating Event Coverage: HP x Media Markt

By Reuben Hester • December 27, 2025

Event coverage often falls into the trap of being a simple recap. When HP and Media Markt Berlin commissioned us to capture their dynamic branded popup, we wanted to avoid the standard "highlight reel" and instead create a piece of cinema that could stand on its own.

Visual Centerpieces

Our focus was to capture the sheer atmosphere of the event, placing artist Bondtrulove as the visual centerpiece of the activation. Instead of just documenting the crowd, we focused on the flow of the experience—how people interacted with the technology and the art.

We documented live moments, genuine crowd engagement, and the artistic elements in a way that felt deliberate. Camera movement was key here; we used stabilization to create smooth, gliding shots that mirrored the premium positioning of the HP products on display.

Polished Vibrancy

The final edit delivered a strong highlight piece that reflects the spirit of the event—a true connection between technology, art, and community. It proves that event videos don't have to be chaotic; they can be vibrant, polished, and narratively driven.
