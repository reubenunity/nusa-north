Scaling Past €50k/Month: The "Creative-First" Strategy

By Reuben Hester • December 26, 2025

In 2026, the algorithm is smart enough to find your audience. The bottleneck is no longer detailed targeting; the bottleneck is your creative asset.

Media Buying is Now Creative Strategy

If you are spending more time inside Facebook Ads Manager than you are developing your creative concepts, you are losing. Algorithms like Meta's Advantage+ have democratized targeting. Now, the ad itself does the targeting.

If you create a video that appeals to luxury travelers, the algorithm will find them. If you create a video that appeals to bargain hunters, it will find them too. Your creative script is your targeting setting.

The High-Volume Myth

There is a misconception that you need to test 50 new creatives a week to scale. For luxury brands, this is dangerous. "Spamming" the timeline with low-quality tests erodes brand equity.

At Nusa & North, we focus on a "Flagship Creative" model. We produce fewer, higher-quality assets that are designed to last for months, not days. By focusing on deep psychological hooks and cinematic execution, we create assets that can handle €10k+ in daily spend without causing ad fatigue.
