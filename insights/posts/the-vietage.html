Visualizing Slow Travel: The Vietage Story

By Reuben Hester • December 26, 2025

In a world obsessed with speed, The Vietage by Anantara offers something rare: the luxury of time. We were invited to create a full visual story that captures the elegance of their luxury railway journey winding between Quy Nhon and Hoi An.

Documenting the Journey

This commission was about more than just a train; it was about the experience of movement. We were tasked with filming and photographing the onboard experience, from the fine dining elements and free-flowing drinks to the incredibly attentive service.

Our goal was to showcase the calm atmosphere of the carriage against the passing Vietnamese countryside. We balanced wide shots that established the context of the journey with tight, intimate detail shots—the pour of a wine glass, the texture of the seats, the smile of a server.

Elevated Narratives

Our job was to present the journey as an "elevated slow travel experience" that perfectly blends premium design, comfort, and authentic Vietnamese charm. The final collection provided The Vietage with polished hero videos, lifestyle moments, and detail-focused stills that are now driving their marketing channels and global brand platforms.
